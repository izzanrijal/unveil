Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Unveil App

What is Unveil App?

+

Unveil is a privacy-focused mobile application designed to empower Gen Z women (18-25) with objective relationship insights through AI-powered conversation analysis. The app helps identify manipulative communication patterns in dating relationships.

How does Unveil App help me analyze my conversations?

+

Unveil uses AI to analyze uploaded chat recordings and voice conversations, identifying patterns of communication that may be manipulative or concerning. The app provides non-judgmental guidance through an AI coach that speaks like a trusted friend.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Absolutely! Unveil is built with privacy as a core principle. All conversation analysis happens locally on your device, and your data never leaves your phone. We don't store your conversations on our servers.

How can I visualize patterns in my relationships?

+

Unveil provides intuitive visualizations that track behavioral patterns over time, helping you see trends and changes in your relationships. These insights enable you to make more informed decisions about your relationships.
